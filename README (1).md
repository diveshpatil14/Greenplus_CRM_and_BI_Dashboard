# Data-Driven Renewable Customer Ecosystem for GreenPlus Energy Ireland

## Project Overview

This project presents a CRM and Business Intelligence transformation solution for GreenPlus Energy Ireland, a fictional renewable electricity provider.

The project addresses key business challenges including customer churn, billing disputes, slow complaint resolution, weak campaign targeting, and lack of real-time management visibility.

The solution combines Salesforce CRM and Power BI dashboards to centralize customer data, automate complaint escalation, analyze customer risk, monitor service performance, and identify renewable partner opportunities.

## Business Problem

GreenPlus Energy Ireland was facing several operational and strategic issues:

- Customer data was scattered over spreadsheets, emails, and manual files
- Customer complaints were handled manually with no structured escalation process
- Billing disputes were increasing and affecting customer trust
- Marketing campaigns were not data-driven
- Management lacked real-time dashboards for performance monitoring
- Potential renewable partner customers were not being identified or tracked

## Project Objectives

The main objectives of this project were to:

- Design a centralized CRM system for customer and service data
- Automate complaint escalation workflows
- Build Power BI dashboards for business performance monitoring
- Analyze customer churn risk and billing dispute patterns
- Track campaign performance and renewable partner conversion
- Support data-driven decision-making for customer retention and strategic growth

## Dataset Design

Mock datasets were created to simulate realistic renewable energy business operations.

The datasets included:

- Customers
- Contracts
- Bills
- Support Tickets
- Campaigns
- Renewable Partners
- Energy Production

The mock data was designed with realistic business logic, including relationships between customer consumption, billing amount, dispute flags, churn risk, complaint history, sustainability score, and renewable partner potential.

## Salesforce CRM Implementation

The Salesforce CRM implementation included:

- Customer and account management
- Contact records linked to customer accounts
- Case management for complaints and service issues
- Opportunity tracking for renewable energy partner leads
- Custom fields for churn risk, sustainability score, billing information, resolution time, escalation status, and partner potential
- Automated escalation workflow for unresolved cases exceeding 48 hours

## Power BI Dashboards

Three Power BI dashboards were developed.

## 1. Customer Risk and Retention Dashboard

This dashboard tracks customer churn risk, high-risk customer segments, regional churn patterns, customer type, and partner potential.

### Key Metrics

- 982 total customers
- 95 high-churn-risk customers
- 9.7% high churn rate
- 46.48 average churn risk score

## 2. Service, Billing and Complaints Dashboard

This dashboard analyzes support tickets, complaint types, billing disputes, escalation patterns, ticket status, and resolution time.

### Key Metrics

- 391 total tickets
- 2.91 average resolution time in days
- 277 escalated tickets
- 472 disputed bills
- 195.80 average bill amount

## 3. Strategic Growth, Campaigns and Renewable Performance Dashboard

This dashboard monitors campaign conversion, contract status, renewable partner pipeline, estimated capacity, and energy output.

### Key Metrics

- 112 positive responses
- 660 active contracts
- 211 active partners
- 3K total estimated capacity
- 4M total energy generated

## Key Insights

- A small but important group of customers were identified as high churn risk and should be prioritized for retention.
- Billing disputes and service complaints were major operational issues affecting customer satisfaction.
- Some high-risk customers also showed strong renewable partner potential, making them strategically important.
- Campaign conversion dropped significantly from initial contact to positive response, showing the need for better targeting and follow-up.
- Renewable partner tracking can help the company move beyond electricity supply and develop a longer-term renewable energy ecosystem.

## Business Impact

This project demonstrates how CRM and BI integration can help an energy provider:

- Improve customer retention
- Reduce complaint handling delays
- Increase billing dispute visibility
- Support better campaign targeting
- Improve management decision-making
- Identify renewable energy growth opportunities
